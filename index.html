About Us

What is ACM?

The aim of the Nazarbayev University ACM Student Chapter is to promote IT in the university and in Kazakhstan. Regularly we are organizing guest lectures, who are working in local or in international companies. Our last major events are Instructional Odyssey, DigiGirlz and Cracking the coding workshop 

Our main event is <HackNU>. This event is being organized annually and in the last time, it was sponsored by BTSDigital, Google, EPAM and Microsoft.

2x Opted the best Club at NU

At the1 the annual fest of the university clubs, our chapter NU ACM was given the best chapter award in 2018, 2019.

<HackNU>

NU ACM HackNU, It is an all time exclusive hackathon where workshops, project tournaments are held while students love to participate in it.

our objectives

Sig

ACM's SIGs - Special Interest Groups - are the communities that develop together in special technical areas. There is no selection process to attend particular SIG - they are free and are being led by enthusiastic students who have some prior experience. We have different SIGs, such as Base, ICPC, CyberSecurity, Machine Learning, Web development, Game Development, Mobile development.

Conferences

International and National conferences are held by ACM-INDIA all over the world for its members. Students can go to the official ACM-INDIA site and register themselves for any conference of their choice.

Tech-Talks

NU ACM Student chapter often organizes guest lectures with IT specialists from international companies, such as Google, Facebook, Microsoft, and local companies.

WorkShops

NU ACM Student Chapter is hosting workshops such as "Design Thinking & Collaborative Problem-Solving Workshop" with professor Widom, "Crush Your Coding Workshop" with Facebook engineers.

events

Events that are organised by NU ACM. Students participate in such events that help in the overall development of their professional career as well as personal life.

seminars

NU ACM organizes seminars conducted by prominent personalities on the latest technology and gadgets in the market. This helps the students to actually get what's going on in the world.
